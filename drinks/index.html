Information for remote workers coming to Koh Lanta, Thailand

Drinks

Craft Beer

Devy Ale Cafe - Best craft beer selection on the island. Located at the end of Saladan road. Great selection of Thai craft beers (brewed in Cambodia/Laos due to Thai laws). Expect 230-240 baht per beer.

Bonnies Deli - Shop that sells a good selection of craft beers.

Wine

Seven Seas at Pimalai Resort - Fine dining with extensive wine list.

Cocktails

Sugggestions welcome!

Nice bars

Pangea Beach Bar - Long Beach. Great cocktails like Pangea punch and mango madness. Stunning sunset views and chill house music.

Why Not Bar - Kantiang Bay. Affordable cocktails, live music and fire shows every night.

Freedom Bar - Klong Khong. Delicious cocktails, fire shows, hammocks. Party nights on Saturdays.

Funky Monkey - Long Beach. One of the biggest bars on the island with karaoke and live music.

Sound Shack Bar - Happy hour 4PM-9PM with cocktails at 100 baht.
