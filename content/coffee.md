## Cafes

[Slow Bar Caffetaria](https://maps.app.goo.gl/9cmLwVcST9AJKMvg7) - the best coffee on the island. Irregular opening hours. Not on Google Maps, but the pin is the correct location. You can see it with Street View.

[Oasis Yoga](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Oasis+Yoga+Bungalows+Koh+Lanta) - sells beans in their shop
